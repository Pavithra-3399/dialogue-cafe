Events & workshops

More than a coffee break.

From BSL taster sessions to the milestones that got Dialogue Café open in the first place.

BSL taster sessions

Regularly hosted, interactive sessions open to anyone on campus — no experience needed. Ask at the counter or check Dialogue Hub's channels for this term's schedule.

Host your event with us

RDCS offers venue hire for meetings, breakfasts, and society gatherings — with Dialogue Café baristas and BSL access built in, not bolted on.

Community & society visits

Bring a class, society, or team down for a guided visit — a short introduction to the café's model plus a chance to place your first order in BSL.

How we got here

Milestones so far

A short timeline of real moments in Dialogue Café's launch.

19 FEB 2025

Public debut

Dialogue Café made its first public appearance at the Newham Chamber of Commerce Big Budget Breakfast.

EARLY 2025

Doors open at RDCS

The café opened within the Royal Docks Centre for Sustainability, UEL Docklands Campus — visited ahead of launch by the Mayor of Newham and the TUC General Secretary.

2025

Five new roles created

Five paid positions opened for Deaf and hard-of-hearing staff, alongside training places for UEL students.

Coming up

Dialogue Express Café

Next on Dialogue Hub's calendar: a converted train carriage on the Carpenters Estate in Stratford, opening as a new community hub in the same Deaf-led spirit as the UEL café.
